Summer Holiday

Coron Island

Being the third largest island, Coron is one of the best places to visit in the Philippines. Towards the centre of the island, you will find limestone peaks of several heights, with caves underneath which one can explore further to have a day full of adventures.

Tagaytay

Out of all the tourist places in the Philippines, Tagaytay is quite popular in scenic beauty and the comfort its weather provides. Being perched at a higher altitude, you can view the famous Taal Lake and the Taal volcano. The Picnic Grove is another place where you can visit for a relaxing meal while marvelling at the far-off cliffs and a clear view of the ocean and some of the islands.

Intramuros

Constructed by the colonial Spanish or more specifically by Miguel Lopez de Legaspi back in 1571, Intramuros is nicknamed as the “Walled City”. This entire place is encompassed by towering sturdy walls, with some being as tall as 6 meters.

Lake Kayangan

Lying in the northern part of the picturesque Palawan Island, Lake Kayangan is considered to be the cleanest lake in Asia. Flaunting cool and clear waters, this lake is incredibly beautiful. Tourists visiting Lake Kayangan can engage in a number of leisure activities, such as swimming, boating, rafting, caving, hiking and diving.

Siargao

Siargao is known to be one of the famous places in the Philippines for surfing. Even though the island is very small, almost like a teardrop, it’s one of the most visited places. The entire island is bordered with white sandy beaches while the turquoise blue water washes off the shoreline. Inland waterfalls and lagoons are also present, which are major relaxing and rejuvenating spots.

Secret Lagoon

The Secret Lagoon is a stunning hideaway lagoon that can only be accessed through a tiny intriguing cave. As you enter the cave on your boat, you will be astounded by the breathtaking towering limestone outcrops. The sharp outcrops are shrouded with lush trees that offer a contrasting appeal to the grey colour of the limestones.
